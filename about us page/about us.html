1-about the faculty:

Republican Decree No. 419 of 1995 was issued to establish a College of Computers and Information at Ain Shams University, and the study began in September 1996. There are all colleges of scientific departments granting a Bachelor’s in Computers and Information in Computer Science and Information Systems and also granting the College of Computers and Information in Computer Systems, Scientific Calculations, Bioinformatics and Software Engineering Artificial intelligence, digital multimedia and cybersecurity. All universities and international scientific societies are familiar with the fact that the focus of information science is the scientific study of how to devise, generate, encode, convert, send, collect, organize, store, retrieve, broadcast, measure, and evaluate information Including sheds light on the multidisciplinary and this is in addition to the presence of many interdisciplinary disciplines and that the study in the departments of computer science, information systems, computer systems and scientific computations constitute academic tracks that engage within the broad path of information science.
